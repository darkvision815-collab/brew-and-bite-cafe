Our Heritage & Promise

Handcrafted with Love, Shared with Joy

Founded by a passionate group of food lovers and coffee geeks, Brew & Bite was born out of a desire to create the perfect neighborhood living room. We work directly with organic small-scale coffee estates to source single-origin Arabica beans, roasted fresh weekly.

Every ingredient in our scratch kitchen is sourced locally, from organic farm-fresh eggs to handcrafted sourdough baked early each morning. Our warm, aesthetic green-and-beige-themed cafe space is styled for pure comfort, complete with fast fiber Wi-Fi.
